Data input

series can be:
– 'x,y' - two lines, first for y, second for x:
15,7,-3,10
-20,-10,0,8.5
– 'categories' - two lines, first for y, second for categories' names:
7,42,13
Monday,Tuesday,Wednesday
– 'values' - only values, one line:
7,42,13

You can put as many series as you want separates by an empty line
